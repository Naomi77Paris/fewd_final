About Nev

Hi and welcome to Nev Fitness. My name is Nev and I offer personalized fitness training services in your home or at a private-gym in Seattle.


My education includes a 4-year bachelors degree in Physical Education from Ege University, where I graduated third in my class. I moved to the United States from my hometown of Izmir, Turkey in 2006.


I have been working as a fitness instruction for over 10 years. I was a college basketball player and basketball coach and have former students that have played for the Turkish national team. My background includes swimming instruction, step-aerobics and dance. I have also appeared on TV for fitness and exercise programming. Personal activities that are important to me include salsa, argentine tango, and crossfit. But nothing fills me with more happiness, joy and love than my 2-year old son Jackson.


Nothing makes me happier than seeing my clients reach their goals and feel good about themselves.
